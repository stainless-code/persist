Pick by sync-vs-async (does it gate UI?), cross-tab needs, and whether you want structured-clone (Set/Map/Date natively).

| Backend              | Sync? | Cross-tab | Structured-clone | Size               | Gate UI? | Subpath                                           |
| -------------------- | ----- | --------- | ---------------- | ------------------ | -------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| IndexedDB            | ‚úó     | ‚úó         | ‚úì                | large              | ‚úì        | `./backends/idb`                                  |
| AsyncStorage (RN)    | ‚úó     | ‚úó         | ‚úó                | large              | ‚úì        | `./backends/async-storage`                        |
| MMKV (RN)            | ‚úì     | ‚úó         | ‚úó                | large              | ‚úó        | `./backends/mmkv`                                 |
| Secure Store (Expo)  | ‚úó     | ‚úó         | ‚úó                | ~2KB/key           | ‚úì        | `./backends/secure-store`                         |
| Node fs              | ‚úó     | ‚úó         | ‚úó                | disk               | ‚úì        | `./backends/node-fs`                              |
| Encrypted (wrapper)  | ‚úó     | ‚úó         | ‚úó                | inherits           | ‚úì        | `./backends/encrypted`                            |
| Compressed (wrapper) | ‚úó     | ‚úó         | ‚úó                | inherits (smaller) | ‚úì        | `./backends/compressed`                           |
| localStorage         | ‚úì     | ‚úì         | ‚úó                | ~5MB               | ‚úó        | core `createJSONStorage`                          |
| sessionStorage       | ‚úì     | ‚úó         | ‚úó                | ~5MB               | ‚úó        | core `createJSONStorage` / `createSessionStorage` |

IDB has no storage events ‚Äî pair `./transport/crosstab` for cross-tab sync. Encrypted/compressed wrappers over `localStorage` also don't receive native `storage` events: `PersistStorage.raw` is the wrapper identity `crossTab` compares, not the inner `localStorage` ‚Äî use `./transport/crosstab` (BroadcastChannel) for cross-tab there too.
